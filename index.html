About

Eminem, whose real name is Marshall Bruce Mathers III, is a renowned American rapper, songwriter, record producer, and actor. Born on October 17, 1972, in St. Joseph, Missouri, he is often considered one of the greatest and most influential artists in the history of hip-hop and popular music.
